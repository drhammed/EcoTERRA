# Ecology Journal Profiles

> Quick reference for scope, formatting, and submission requirements of major ecology journals.

## British Ecological Society (BES)

| Journal | Scope | Word Limit | Abstract | Reference Style |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Methods in Ecology and Evolution** | Novel methods, software, statistical tools | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Journal of Applied Ecology** | Applied conservation and management | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Journal of Animal Ecology** | Animal ecology at all scales | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Journal of Ecology** | Plant ecology, vegetation, ecosystems | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Functional Ecology** | Functional traits, ecophysiology | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Ecological Solutions and Evidence** | Applied, solution-oriented (OA) | 7000 | 350 words | Author-date |

**BES common requirements**: Double-spaced, line-numbered, figures at end or embedded, data archiving required, code availability encouraged.

## Ecological Society of America (ESA)

| Journal | Scope | Word Limit | Abstract | Reference Style |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Ecology** | Broad ecology, fundamental research | 5000–8000 | 250 words | Author-date |
| **Ecological Monographs** | Comprehensive, synthetic studies | 15000+ | 350 words | Author-date |
| **Ecological Applications** | Applied ecology, conservation | 5000–8000 | 250 words | Author-date |
| **Ecosphere** | Broad ecology (OA) | 10000 | 250 words | Author-date |

## Figure Requirements (Common)

| Requirement | Typical Standard |
|---|---|
| Resolution | ≥ 300 DPI (600 for line art) |
| Single column width | 80–90 mm |
| Double column width | 170–180 mm |
| File formats | PDF, TIFF, EPS (submission); PNG for review |
| Color model | CMYK for print, RGB for online |
